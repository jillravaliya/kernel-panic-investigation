=== System Configuration ===

This proves that on NVMe systems, the driver is module-only (=m).
Without initrd, the kernel cannot access the root filesystem.

--- Current Kernel ---
6.17.0-14-generic

--- NVMe Driver Configuration ---
CONFIG_BLK_DEV_NVME=m

CONFIG_BLK_DEV_NVME=m means "module" - driver is in initrd, not built into kernel
CONFIG_BLK_DEV_NVME=y would mean "built-in" - would work without initrd

--- Postinst Script Execution Order (alphabetical) ---
dkms
initramfs-tools
unattended-upgrades
update-notifier
xx-update-initrd-links
zz-shim
zz-update-grub

Notice: "dkms" runs before "initramfs-tools" alphabetically.
When dkms fails, run-parts stops, initramfs-tools never runs.
